Hello, world!

Welcome to interviewCreator(iC).
iC lets you schedule interviews between participants, edit already created interviews and view your upcoming interviews.
For more info on how to use iC refer below.

  • For creating an interview click on Create Interview in the navbar above and fill out the given fileds.
  • For viewing upcoming interviews click on Interviews in the navbar above.
  • For editing interviews, head over to Interviews section, click on the Interview you want to edit to view its details, and then click on Edit Interview.
  • For viewing all the participants info click on Participants in the navbar above.
